Entry II — LUNAR AMULET - Yoke of Oblivion (LUNAR AMULET)

This is a track by LUNAR AMULET, taken from the self-titled release. The project operates in the raw, atmospheric margins of black metal — distant production, suffocating space, and repetition used as ritual rather than structure.

“Yoke of Oblivion” unfolds slowly and without relief. Guitars hang in the air, percussion moves like erosion, and the overall weight of the recording replaces clarity with isolation. Nothing is polished. Nothing reaches for effect.

This is not music for consumption. It is a document of atmosphere and discipline, indifferent to comfort, resistant to interpretation, and hostile to compromise.
