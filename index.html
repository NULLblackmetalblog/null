entry i — manifesto

NULL exists for one purpose: raw black metal.

No post-black metal. No blackgaze. No symphonic excess. No crossover, fusion, or aesthetic dilution.

This is not a platform for trends, revival scenes, marketing language, or the softening of a form that was never meant to be comfortable.

We document recordings born from isolation, discipline, necessity, and hostility toward compromise.

Anything that does not embody that spirit has no place here.

There is no center.
